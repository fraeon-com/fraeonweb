What is No Man's Sky

If don't already know what this game is about (how have you not heard of it!?) No Man's Sky is an exploration/survival/space sim in a huge procedurally generated universe. What really caught the attention of gamers everywhere was the sheer size of No Man's Sky's universe which has over 18 quintillion planets each of which you fly to and explore every inch of. You'll start off with a ship some basic equipment and then it's up to you what happens next, whether you simply explore aimlessly, head to the mysterious center of the universe, or look for any signs of alien civilizations to trade with. There are no preset goals or ways to "win" the game
